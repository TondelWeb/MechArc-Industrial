Service 01

Industrial Equipment Repair

When equipment goes down, the clock starts ticking. We provide hands-on diagnostic and repair services for a wide range of industrial machinery and components. Our 30+ years of experience means we can identify problems quickly and fix them properly.

We work on components across manufacturing, facilities, plant operations, and commercial environments. Whatever the issue, we'll tell you straight what it needs and get it done.

  • Mechanical component diagnosis and repair
  • Motors, drives, and power transmission systems
  • Gearboxes, pumps, and hydraulic components
  • Custom solutions for non-standard equipment
  • Documentation and repair reporting available

REBUILD
Service 02

Parts Refurbishment & Rebuilding

Replacing industrial parts can be expensive and slow — especially for older or specialized equipment. We rebuild and refurbish worn or damaged components to like-new specifications, giving you a cost-effective alternative to full replacement.

Parts are inspected, reconditioned, and tested before they're returned. The goal is simple: send it to us beat up, get it back ready to run.

  • Full disassembly, inspection, and cleaning
  • Bearing, seal, and wear part replacement
  • Surface restoration and component machining
  • Quality-tested before return shipment
  • Cost-effective vs. OEM replacement parts

Preventative Maintenance

The cheapest repair is the one you never have to make. Scheduled maintenance and inspections are the most reliable way to extend equipment life and avoid unplanned downtime.

We'll work with you to establish a maintenance schedule that fits your operation — checking wear points, flagging developing issues, and keeping things running before small problems become big ones.

  • Scheduled inspection programs
  • Wear and fatigue assessment
  • Lubrication, adjustment, and minor servicing
  • Written reports and recommendations
  • Flexible scheduling around your production hours

Who We Are

MechArc Industrial is an industrial repair and rebuilding company serving businesses across Virginia and North Carolina. We specialize in equipment repair, parts refurbishment, belt manufacturing, and general mechanical services — the kind of work that keeps facilities running and production on schedule.

What sets us apart isn't complicated: we show up, we do the work right, and we stand behind what we deliver. After more than 30 years in this industry, we've built our reputation one repair at a time. Our customers come back because we're reliable, straightforward, and good at what we do.

Most of our work involves components that can be shipped to our facility, serviced, and returned — so geography rarely gets in the way. Whether you're a large manufacturing plant or a smaller commercial operation, we treat every job with the same level of care.

We're not chasing every corner of the market. We focus on doing a specific set of things exceptionally well, and doing them for businesses that depend on having a repair partner they can count on.
